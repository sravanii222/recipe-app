Boneless Fish Fry | Amritsari Fish Fry | Ajwain Fish Fry recipe | How to Make Boneless Fish Fry

Boneless Fish Fry | Amritsari Fish Fry | Ajwain Fish Fry recipe- The very mention of Fish Fry makes our mouths water. And Amritsari Fish Fry is by far the best. There are numerous kinds of Fish Fry, but the Amritsari version is fragrant with Ajwain and tasty.

Tips:

* Fish: Amritsari Fish Fry is a Boneless Fish Fry. Those who like can use Fish with scales.

*I have used the Rohu fish. You could use any Boneless Fish.

* After coating the Fish with spices, marinate for at least an hour to ensure the flavours stick.

* Oil should be enough for the Fish to get fully immersed.

Ingredients:

*400 gms Boneless Rohu Fish

* 1 tsp Lemon Juice

* 1 tsp Ginger Garlic Paste

* 1 tsp Chopped Green Coriander

*Salt

*1/2 tsp Ajwain

*3/4 tsp Roasted Cumin Powder

*1/2 tsp Garam Masala

* 1 tsp Mirchi Powder

*1/8 tsp Turmeric

*3-4 tbsps Bengal Gram Flour

*1 tbsp Rice Flour

* 3-4 tbsps Water

*Oil to Fry

Instructions:

Coat the Fish pieces with all the spice and marinate for an hour.
