Urad dal ladoo recipe | urad dal laddu | udad ke laddu | urad ki laddu

Tips:

Ingredents:

. 1 cup rice

.Besan Flour½ cup

.Powdered Sugar¼

. Ghee (cup + 2 tbsp)

.¼ teaspoonCardamom Powder

. 6 strandsSaffron

.2 tablespoonsCashews (broken)

Instructions:

.firstly, in a pan take 1½ cup urad dal.

.roast on low flame until the dal turns aromatic.

.keep roasting the dal for 10 minutes, or until it turns golden brown.

. cool completely, and transfer to a mixi.

.blend to fine powder without adding any water.

. sieve the flour to prevent from any lumps.

. now in a small mixi, take ½ cup sugar and blend to a fine powder.

.add in powdered sugar to the same urad dal powder.

.also, add ½ tsp cardamom powder and mix well making sure everything is combined well. further, take ¼ cup ghee in a saucepan.

.add 2 tbsp raisins and fry on low flame until raisins puff up.

.add in fried raisins along with ghee to the same urad dal flour mixture.

.mix well forming a moist mixture.

.if the mixture is dry, add a tbsp of more melted ghee. and if the mixture turns watery, then allow to rest for 10 minutes, so it absorbs and thickens.

. grease hands with ghee and prepare a ball sized ladoo.

. finally, enjoy urad dal ladoo for 2 weeks when stored in an airtight container.
