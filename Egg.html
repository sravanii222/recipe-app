Crispy Egg Bonda | Egg Bajji | Egg Pakoda

Tips:

Thickness of Batter:

1.Check the thickness of the batter by dipping your finger. It should stay on the finger for some time which indicates that the egg can hold the batter

I have not used Rice flour.You can use it to get crispy ones.

Frying tips

1.See to it that the Egg is completely covered with the batter because there are chances of the Egg bursting in the oil

Ingredients:

6 Boiled Eggs

3/4 cup Gram Flour

1/8 tsp Turmeric

Salt

2 Cooking Soda

1/4 tsp Cumin

1 tsp Finely Chopped Green Chilli

1 tsp Chopped Coriander

1/2 tsp Chilli powder

1/8 tsp Chat masala

1/4 tsp Garam masala

1/4 tsp Roasted Cumin powder

Water-As Required

Instructions:

Cut the boiled eggs into half and keep them aside.

Make a thick batter of Bengal Gram flour using all the ingredients

Dip the half cut Eggs carefully into the batter and fry till they turn golden brown.

Garnish it with Chat masala and finely chopped Onions and serve hot.
