Perugu Pachchadi Recipe

Tips:

Ingredents:

300 grams Yoghurt (curd, beaten)

200 gramsPotato Boiled (chopped into cubes)

100 Mililitre Water

2 pinches

Salt½ teaspoon

Mustard Seeds½ teaspoon

Cumin Seeds2 numbers

Red Chilli (halved) ½ teaspoon

Urad Dal

5 leavesCurry Leaves

1 pinch

Asafoetida 2 teaspoons

Refined Oil

Instructions:

Make this easy recipe by beating the curd like you beat an egg in a large bowl. Add a little water and salt, mix them together and make a fine batter.

Take boiled and chopped potatoes and add it to the curd mix. Toss them well.

Heat oil in a pan over low-medium flame and add mustard seeds, asafoetida, cumin seeds, urad dal and halved red chillies in the oil.

As soon as the mustard seeds start to crackle, add curry leaves and remove the tadka from the flame.

Transfer the tadka to the curd-vegetable bowl and mix it well.

Sprinkle a pinch of salt as per taste and serve as a side dish with rice and rasam.
